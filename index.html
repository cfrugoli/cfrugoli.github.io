This is my MIDI Effect! You can transpose your notes easily and quickly with the provided slider. You can also use the provided drop-down menu to choose from a predetermined list of chord types. This function will take the note you play and consider it the root note of whatever chord you choose. You will then hear that full chord as you play only one note on the keyboard! Have fun and enjoy!
